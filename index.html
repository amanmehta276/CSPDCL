Substation Summary

The Nawapara Rajim 33/11 kV Substation is operated by Chhattisgarh State Power Distribution Company Limited (CSPDCL) under the Rajim Division, Raipur Circle. Commissioned in 2010, it plays a vital role in distributing power to Nawapara Rajim and surrounding rural and urban areas.


The substation is equipped with two 5 MVA power transformers operating at 33/11 kV, with three incoming feeders sourced from major grid substations and thirteen outgoing feeders serving residential, agricultural, commercial, and industrial consumers.
